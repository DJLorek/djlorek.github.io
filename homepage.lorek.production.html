Music Production
 
 
Since 2017, I have been active as a music producer,
starting from a fully self-taught and experimental approach of personal exploration of style.
My studies in Goody Music DJ School provided me not only
the theoretical foundations of my DJing Skills but also my production ones.
Over time, I released several unofficial remixes on YouTube that reached over 10,000 views.
at the same time i worked with other producers from
completely different genres, which helped me building
even stronger versatility as an artist.
 
Today my works are a big mix of all my musical experiences,
shaping a personal and recognizable sound.
